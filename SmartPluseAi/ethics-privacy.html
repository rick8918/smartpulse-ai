Ethics & Privacy

Our commitment to responsible AI development and user privacy is fundamental to our mission.

Our Ethical Principles

We've built SmartPulse AI on a foundation of ethical guidelines that prioritize user wellbeing and autonomy.

Privacy First

All data processing happens locally on your device. We never sell, share, or monetize your personal data.

  • On-device processing only
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • End-to-end encryption for stored data

Transparency

We clearly explain how our AI works, what data we collect, and how we use it to make predictions.

  • Clear explanations of AI decisions
  • Accessible privacy policy
  • Open documentation of algorithms

User Autonomy

SmartPulse AI provides suggestions, not commands. You always maintain full control over your decisions.

  • Opt-in for all features
  • Easy to disable interventions
  • User controls all data collection

Beneficence

Our AI is designed to promote user wellbeing, not to maximize engagement or time spent with our product.

  • Wellbeing-focused design
  • No dark patterns
  • Evidence-based interventions

Privacy-First Architecture

Data Collection

User activity data is collected locally on the device with explicit user permission. Only anonymized, aggregated behavioral patterns are processed.

Local Processing

All AI processing happens on your device. No personal data is sent to external servers. The model learns and improves without compromising privacy.

Personalized Insights

AI generates personalized behavior insights and suggestions that remain on your device. You choose what to do with these insights.

Zero Data Transmission

Unlike many AI systems that send your data to cloud servers for processing, SmartPulse AI keeps everything on your device. This means:

  • No risk of data breaches from external servers
  • Complete control over your personal information
  • Functionality even without internet connection
  • No third-party access to your behavioral data

Transparency Panel

We believe in being open about how our AI systems work and make decisions.

Algorithmic Transparency

Our machine learning models are based on interpretable architectures that allow us to explain why certain predictions are made. We avoid "black box" models in favor of transparent algorithms that can be audited and understood.

  • Decision trees for pattern recognition
  • Rule-based systems for intervention timing
  • Regular algorithmic audits by third parties

Data Usage Disclosure

We clearly document what data we collect, how we use it, and how long we retain it. Users can access, export, or delete their data at any time through the app settings.

  • Activity timestamps (not content)
  • App usage patterns (not specific actions)
  • Behavioral sequences (not personal identifiers)
  • All data deleted upon app uninstallation

User Control Settings

Every user has complete control over their SmartPulse AI experience. You can customize what data is collected, what types of interventions you receive, and when the AI is active.

  • Granular permission controls
  • Scheduled "AI-free" periods
  • Custom intervention sensitivity
  • One-click data export and deletion
